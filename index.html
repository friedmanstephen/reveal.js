02  ·  THE STRATEGIC SHIFT
studio-t · June 2026
FROM TOOL TO PARTNER
The pedagogical surface has moved.
AI is no longer a chatbot students consult. It is an autonomous agent that researches, drafts, critiques, and completes coursework end-to-end.
YESTERDAY'S MODEL
Fragmented AI Tools

Faculty and students each adopt isolated chat assistants, with no shared standards or oversight.

  • Consumer chatbots used reactively, one prompt at a time
  • No persistent context, no institutional memory
  • Detection arms race; integrity policed after the fact
  • Curriculum unchanged; pedagogy unchanged
THE AGENTIC ERA
Integrated Agentic Workflows

An institutional system where AI agents act as designed pedagogical partners — scoped, audited, owned.

  • Multi-step agents that plan, retrieve, draft, and verify
  • Embedded in assignments — pedagogy redesigned, not patched
  • Integrity by design, surfaced through artifact + process trails
  • Aligned with Harvard / Ajjan frameworks for agentic learning
